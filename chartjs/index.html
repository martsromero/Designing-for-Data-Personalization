#PhVote2016


The survey fieldwork was conducted from September 8 to 14, 2015 using face-to-face interviews.

This nationwide survey is based on a sample of 2,400 representative adults 19 years old and above. It has a +/- 2% error margin at the 95% confidence level. Subnational estimates for each of the geographic areas covered in the survey (i.e. Metro Manila, the rest of Luzon, Visayas and Mindanao) have a +/- 4% error margin, also at 95% confidence level.
